# Charlotte Food Tour Simulator

Welcome to the Queen City! This interactive Python-based text adventure lets you explore Charlotte, North Carolina through its vibrant food scene. Choose your destinations, make delicious decisions, and discover local favorites — all from the comfort of your terminal.

## How to Play

1. Open this repo in **GitHub Codespaces**
2. Once the Codespace loads, open the terminal
3. Run the game with:

```bash
python3 CharlotteAdventureGame.py
Follow the prompts and enjoy the ride!

Destinations You Can Explore

Metro Diner
